Netanyahu meets Trump amid growing probes for both
US President Donald Trump is considering attending the opening of the American embassy in Jerusalem in May.

It is a controversial move that could further inflame tensions over peace negotiations with Palestine.

Trump has been holding talks with Israel's Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu at the White House - their fifth meeting in a year.

Al Jazeera's Kimberly Halkett reports from Washington, DC.
