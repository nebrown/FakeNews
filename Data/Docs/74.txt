How to Write a Sitcom “The Long Way”
Greg's talks to Hollywood writer and producer Rob Long. Rob started his career as a screenwriter for the TV show Cheers and is now the Executive Producer for Kevin Can Wait. He is a regular writer for National Review, Newsweek International, and the Los Angeles Times. His weekly commentary "Martini Shot" is broadcast on L.A.'s KCRW and can be found on iTunes. Long is also editor-in-chief of Ricochet.com. Rob gives his tips for sitcom writing and Greg pitches Rob some unique show ideas.
