Canadian Olympian Explains Why Wearing #7 Is So Important To Her

On the ice at the Pyeongchang Olympics, Canadian hockey forward Laura Stacey will wear the same number her great-grandfather, King Clancy, wore in the NHL over 80 years ago. Stacey says she’s always wanted to “follow in his footsteps.”
