Hogan Gidley On President Obama’s Claims His Administration Was Scandal Free
Audio clip: Listen to audio clip.

Hogan Gidley, Special Assistant to President Trump & White House Deputy Press Secretary, spoke with Brian Kilmeaade about President Trump's listening sessions on gun violence, the Supreme Court's DACA decision and President Obama saying there were no scandals in his administration. Gidley said the reason it did not seem like there were scandals in the Obama administration is because the Press were "willing or unwitting accomplices" who were "willing to do or say whatever the President (Obama) puts out." Gidley went on to say that President Obama "never received the level of scrutiny" President Trump has from the press.
