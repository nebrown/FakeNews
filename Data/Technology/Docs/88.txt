Eye Candy Everywhere

Geneva, Switzerland, is the only European city to host a major auto show every year, and it is always chock full of amazing machinery. With models on display ranging from electric SUVs to ultra-luxury convertibles to exotic high-performance machines, the Geneva show captures the imagination and commands attention.
